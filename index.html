Epic Pong!

Weclome to the best version of Pong ever created!

Epic Pong is a recreation of the classic video game Pong created with Javascript and HTML

In case you were born under a rock, this game has two paddles and a ball. You get a point when you get the ball past the other player's paddle. The winner is the first to 11 points.

Hit the spacebar to begin.

Enjoy!
